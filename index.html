General Knowledge

Field-tested tips

💧

Irrigation Timing

Water in the early morning or evening to cut evaporation loss by up to 30%.

🌡️

Heat Stress Watch

Above 38 °C, shield young seedlings with shade nets and increase watering frequency.

🍂

Post-Rain Soil Care

After heavy rain, loosen topsoil gently to prevent crusting and help root respiration.

🐛

Humidity & Pest Pressure

High humidity (>80 %) increases fungal risk. Apply neem-based sprays as a preventive.

🌬️

Windy Conditions

Winds above 30 km/h can lodge tall crops. Stake plants and delay spraying to avoid drift.

📅

Crop Rotation Benefit

Rotating legumes with cereals restores soil nitrogen and breaks pest-disease cycles naturally.
